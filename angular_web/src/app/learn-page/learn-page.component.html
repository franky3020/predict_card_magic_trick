How to performance this magic?

First, magician open this App, and click the "Start" button secretly then put your phone on the table, like nothing happened.

Magician tell the audience that "Choose a number between 11 ~ 13 through the gesture with your fingers" and "Choose a suit through the gesture with your hand", (at here you can explanation how to make a "Spades" shape by hand, "Heart" shape by hand ...) After audience choosed the card, you can let her watch your phone, and wipe off the black screen rapidly. In the end the card was chosen by audience will appear on the screen.
